Hello!

Welcome to HeritageHill Hotel, a haven where the echoes of history resonate harmoniously with contemporary comfort. Nestled in the embrace of a time-honored castle's shadow, our hotel stands as a gateway to a realm where past and present converge in seamless harmony.

A Tapestry of Heritage

Embark on a journey with us, where each brick of the HeritageHill tells a story of medieval grandeur. Our hotel, formally known as Lord Arnold's Castle, draws inspiration from the rich tapestry of the past. The architecture, a blend of contemporary sophistication and timeless elegance, pays homage to the majestic history that surrounds us.
